# ☁️ Deploying with Sevalla

Larasonic can be deployed like any standard Laravel/Octane application on Sevalla. Deploying apps on Sevalla is really easy and simple.

Sevalla is the hosting sponsor for Larasonic, and our [Demo App](https://larasonic.com) is hosted on Sevalla.
